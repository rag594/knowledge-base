## About

Bohan Zhang is a Member of Technical Staff at OpenAI, working on production database infrastructure. He co-authored "The Part of PostgreSQL We Hate the Most" with Prof. Andy Pavlo at Carnegie Mellon University — cited in the PostgreSQL Wikipedia page. His writing focuses on large-scale systems engineering with PostgreSQL.
